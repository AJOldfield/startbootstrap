About Share Your Farming Gear




Share Your Farming Gear exists to help Australian farmers and contractors increase profits through increased machinery utilisation and more efficient access to rental machinery and contractors. It has five main features:

  1. An efficient national marketplace for machinery rental and contractor hire
  2. Intelligent matching of farmers with compatible schedules who could profitably share machinery
  3. Automated administration for all renting, sharing and contracting
  4. The provision of insurance to guarantee machinery availability
  5. A market for sharing trusted labour.
