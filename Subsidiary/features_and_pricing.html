Features and Pricing

Machinery and Conracting Listing and Hiring

The core feature of Share Your Farming Gear is a searchable online database of machinery and contracting services. It is free to list or search for machinery and services on the database. This allows anyone to simply compare their current machinery costs with alternatives available through our system.
We only charge when a user (you!) want to connect with the lister of a machine or service. The price for each connect is below.

Insurance

Two of the greatest objections to renting or sharing machinery is the risk that a machine - even your own machine - is not available for your operation when it is required. A key supporting offering of Share Your Farming Gear is that we can insure you against this risk, or the risk of damage to your machine.
